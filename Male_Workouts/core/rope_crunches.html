Rope Crunches

A rope crunch is an abdominal exercise that uses a rope attachment on a cable machine. Unlike regular crunches, you perform this exercise while kneeling and pulling down against the weighted cable for resistance.

3 sets of 10 to 12 repetitions
Target Muscle:
Instructions:
1. Kneeling on the ground facing the high pulley, grasp one end of the rope in each hand and bring them to the height of your head.
2. Bend your torso so that it is almost parallel to the ground. Contract your abs by bringing your elbows back towards your knees.
3. Stay in the contraction position for a while before slowly returning to the original position. Repeat.
Benefits: The cable crunch allows you to challenge your abs not only by doing more reps, but by gradually lifting heavier each week.
