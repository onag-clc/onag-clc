EZ Bar Preacher Curls

The EZ bar preacher curl is a great exercise to isolate the biceps, while minimizing the strain on your wrists.

3 sets of 8 to 12 repetitions
Target Muscle:
Instructions:
1. Lie face down on an exercise mat with hands should be shoulder-width apart; palms flat on the floor.
2. Get up in a push up position, bend your elbows and rest your weight on your forearms. Your body should form a straight line from your shoulders to your ankles.
3. Be careful not to fully extend your forearms in the lowered position to avoid putting too much strain on the elbow joint. Repeat.
Benefits: One of the best moves to build those bicep peaks is the preacher curl. It's a move that, when done correctly, forces your bicep to work in near-full isolation.
