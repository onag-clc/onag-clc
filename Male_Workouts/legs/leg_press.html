Leg Press

The leg press is a popular piece of gym equipment that can help build key muscles in your legs.

3 sets of 10 to 12 repetitions
Target Muscle:
Instructions:
1. Sit on the machine with your back and head resting comfortably against the padded support. Place your feet on the footplate about hip-width apart while ensuring that your heels are flat.
2. Extend your legs slowly and keep your head and back flat against the seat pad.
3. Pause at the top of the movement. Do not lock out your knees, return the footplate to the starting position by gradually bending the knees. Repeat.
Benefits: It can emphasize different muscles by varying your foot position. It builds strength in these muscles, and you can use it to overcome imbalances.
